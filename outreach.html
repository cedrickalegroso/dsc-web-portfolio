About the Outreach Activity

The Tubigan Elementary School Outreach Activity was held on January 12, 2021, with its participants gathered at the Zarraga Municipal Hall. The goal of this outreach was to provide some alleviation to the difficulties that many educational institutions face in the context of a community quarantine.
Due to the complications and limitations brought about by the lockdown caused by the covid-19 pandemic, the forms of outreach that the organizations of CPU are capable of organizing are now limited to those that have the least amount of social contact. Even though we cannot hold physical events as safely as in the past, we can still provide aid even with distancing protocols, which in this case, is through donations.
