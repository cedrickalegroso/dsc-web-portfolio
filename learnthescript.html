About this event

The Developer Student Club Central Philippine University presents the second workshop under our Web Development Series - an event that will span three weekends and will give most people the kickstart they need to get into web development. This workshop in particular presents the basics of adding interactivity to one's web pages before delving into more intermediate stuff, such as servers and security.
