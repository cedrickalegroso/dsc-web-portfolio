About this event

Design from Scratch with Edwin Capalla was the first installment of the Fundamentals of Design series that aimed to help attendees, particularly beginners, grasp the basic concepts of design such as line, shape, hierarchy, contrast, and composition. In addition, the speaker also tackled color theory and provided resources for participants to implement what they have learned.
